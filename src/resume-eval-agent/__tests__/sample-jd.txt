JOB DESCRIPTION: Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Company: TechFin Bank
Location: Remote

---

JOB TITLE: Senior Machine Learning Engineer

ABOUT THE ROLE

We are looking for an experienced Senior Machine Learning Engineer to lead ML initiatives in our
Banking and Financial Services division. You will architect and build scalable AI/ML solutions that
directly impact millions of customers across our platform.

---

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

• Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for fraud detection, risk assessment, and
  customer analytics in a banking environment
  
• Lead end-to-end ML projects from concept to production, including data engineering, model training,
  evaluation, and monitoring
  
• Mentor junior machine learning engineers and foster best practices in the ML team
  
• Collaborate with product, data, and engineering teams to understand requirements and translate them
  into technical solutions
  
• Optimize model performance and inference latency for real-time applications
  
• Ensure compliance with banking regulations and data privacy standards

---

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS (MANDATORY)

• 4+ years of professional experience as a Machine Learning Engineer
• Proficiency in Python and SQL for data processing and model development
• Strong experience with machine learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn)
• Experience building and deploying ML models in production environments
• Solid understanding of banking domain and financial services
• AWS or Cloud platform experience (EC2, S3, or equivalent services)
• Strong communication and leadership skills

---

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

• Experience with Docker and Kubernetes for model deployment
• Knowledge of Deep Learning and neural networks
• Experience with LLMs and Generative AI applications
• Distributed computing experience (Spark, Hadoop)
• Experience with MLOps and model monitoring frameworks
• Advanced certification in AWS or cloud platforms
• Master's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field

---

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

• Strong problem-solving skills and ability to think creatively
• Detail-oriented with a passion for data quality and model interpretability
• Experience working in Agile/Scrum environments
• Comfortable with ambiguity and rapidly changing priorities
• Commitment to continuous learning and staying current with AI/ML trends

- Cloud: AWS (Primary), Azure (Secondary)
- Databases: PostgreSQL, DynamoDB
- Tools: Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Airflow
- Domain: Banking, Financial Services, Risk Management
