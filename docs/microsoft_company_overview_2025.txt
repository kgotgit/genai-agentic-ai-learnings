Microsoft Corporation – Company Overview (2025)

1.  Basic Information Company Name: Microsoft Corporation Founded: April
    4, 1975 Founders: Bill Gates and Paul Allen Headquarters: Redmond,
    Washington, United States CEO (2025): Satya Nadella Industry:
    Software, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Consumer
    Electronics

Microsoft is one of the largest technology companies in the world, known
for products such as Windows operating systems, Microsoft Office, Azure
cloud services, Xbox gaming systems, and enterprise software solutions.

2.  History of Microsoft

1975–1985: Foundation and Early Growth Microsoft was founded in 1975 by
Bill Gates and Paul Allen in Albuquerque, New Mexico. The company
initially developed software for early personal computers, starting with
a version of the BASIC programming language for the Altair 8800.

In 1980, Microsoft partnered with IBM to supply the MS‑DOS operating
system for IBM personal computers. This partnership accelerated
Microsoft’s growth and helped establish its dominance in the PC software
market.

1985–2000: Windows and Office Era Microsoft launched Windows in 1985 as
a graphical interface for MS‑DOS. Over time, Windows became the dominant
operating system for personal computers worldwide.

In the early 1990s Microsoft introduced Microsoft Office, a productivity
suite including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. It quickly became the
standard productivity software for businesses globally.

2000–2014: Diversification Microsoft expanded into several new markets
including: - Gaming (Xbox console launched in 2001) - Enterprise server
software - Online services - Search engine (Bing)

2014–Present: Cloud and AI Transformation When Satya Nadella became CEO
in 2014, Microsoft shifted its focus toward cloud computing and
artificial intelligence.

Major developments include: - Rapid growth of Microsoft Azure -
Expansion of enterprise cloud services - Integration of AI
technologies - Strategic investments and partnerships in AI research

3.  Microsoft Revenue (Fiscal Year 2025)

Commercial Products - Windows Operating Systems - LinkedIn - Xbox and

5.  Global Presence and Locations

centers, and cloud data centers worldwide.

Major locations include: - Redmond, Washington (Global Headquarters) -
Mountain View, California (Engineering and AI) - New York, USA

Bangalore, Noida (India development centers) - Dublin, Ireland (European
headquarters) - Singapore (Asia Pacific regional hub) - Beijing, China
(Research center)
