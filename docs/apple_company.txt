Apple Company Overview (2025)

1. Introduction

Apple Inc. is one of the world’s largest technology companies. It
designs and manufactures consumer electronics, software, and online
services. Major products include the iPhone, Mac computers, iPad
tablets, Apple Watch, AirPods, and services such as Apple Music, iCloud,
and Apple TV+.

2. Company History

Apple was founded on April 1, 1976, by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and
Ronald Wayne in Cupertino, California, USA. The company initially
focused on personal computers such as the Apple I and Apple II. Later,
Apple introduced groundbreaking products including: - Macintosh (1984) -
iPod (2001) - iPhone (2007) - iPad (2010)

These innovations transformed the global consumer technology industry.

3. Headquarters and Global Presence

Apple’s headquarters is located at Apple Park in Cupertino, California,
USA.

The company has offices, research centers, and retail stores across many
countries including: - United States - India - China - United Kingdom -
Germany - Singapore - Japan - Australia - Canada

Apple products are sold in more than 100 countries worldwide.

4. Employees

As of 2025, Apple employs approximately 160,000+ employees globally.
Employees work in areas such as engineering, product design, software
development, retail operations, marketing, and corporate functions.

5. Revenue and Financial Performance (2025)

Key estimated financial figures for 2025:

Total Revenue: Around $410+ billion Net Income: Around $110+ billion

Major revenue sources: - iPhone - Mac - iPad - Wearables (Apple Watch,
AirPods) - Services (App Store, Apple Music, iCloud, Apple TV+, Apple
Pay)

6. Major Product Categories

Apple’s main product ecosystem includes:

1.  iPhone – Smartphones
2.  Mac – Laptops and desktops
3.  iPad – Tablets
4.  Apple Watch – Smartwatch
5.  AirPods – Wireless earphones
6.  Apple Services – Digital ecosystem

The iPhone remains the company’s largest revenue contributor.

7. Market Position

Apple is consistently ranked among the most valuable companies in the
world. Its market capitalization has crossed several trillion dollars,
making it a global leader in consumer technology.

8. Future Focus

Apple continues investing in:

-   Artificial Intelligence
-   Augmented Reality (AR)
-   Custom Apple Silicon chips
-   Health and wearable technology
-   Cloud and digital services

The company is also expanding manufacturing and supply chain operations
in countries such as India.
